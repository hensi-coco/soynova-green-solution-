Our Story

We See Value Where Others See Waste

Soynova Green Solution LLP was founded with a simple belief: everyday products can be beautiful, practical, and gentle on the planet at the same time.

From Fields to Tableware

Across soybean‑growing regions, tons of stems, husks, and stalks are left over after harvest. Much of this residue is burned in open fields, sending carbon and particulates into the air. Our founders, with backgrounds in agriculture and materials science, saw an opportunity to turn this underused resource into something purposeful.

After years of research and pilot production, Soynova developed a process that transforms soybean plant waste into strong, food‑safe paper plates and bowls. Every product we make is a small step away from landfills and towards a circular economy.

Why Soybean Plant Waste?

Soybean residue is abundant, renewable, and naturally fibrous — making it an ideal base material for molded tableware. By using an agricultural by‑product instead of cutting new trees or relying on petroleum‑based plastics, we dramatically lower the footprint of every plate and bowl we produce.

Working directly with farming communities, we source and process crop residue in a way that adds value to rural economies and reduces open‑field burning. Sustainability for us is not a label; it is a supply chain decision we make every day.

Our Sustainability Mission

Our mission is to make plant‑based, single‑use tableware the default choice for food service and events. We focus on three areas:

  • Reducing dependence on plastic and foam disposables through practical, affordable alternatives.
  • Closing material loops by designing products that safely return to the soil after use.
  • Supporting farmers and rural partners by creating new value from crop residue.

Looking Ahead

We envision a future where packaging and tableware no longer compete with forests or pollute oceans. Instead, they are made from smartly used agricultural by‑products that regenerate the land when they break down.

As we grow, we continue to refine our processes, lower emissions, and expand our product range. But our compass remains the same: create honest, thoughtfully designed products that help businesses and families choose sustainability without sacrificing convenience or aesthetics.
