Turning Farm Residue into Everyday Essentials

Every harvest leaves behind soybean stems and husks that are usually burned or left to rot. We give this plant waste a second life as safe, food‑grade plates and bowls that replace single‑use plastics.

Our manufacturing process is designed to be low‑waste, energy efficient, and aligned with circular economy principles. The result: eco‑friendly tableware that feels premium in hand, yet returns gently to the earth when its job is done.

Why Choose Soynova?

Made from Soybean Waste

We upcycle soybean crop residue, reducing the need for virgin wood pulp and preventing open‑field burning.

Plastic-Free & Compostable

Our plates and bowls are free from plastics and toxic coatings, and are designed to safely break down after use.

Reliable for Real‑World Use

Strong, heat‑resistant, and grease‑resistant — ideal for food courts, catering, corporate events, and eco‑conscious homes.

Partner to Conscious Businesses

We work closely with restaurants, caterers, and retailers to supply consistent, scalable, and branded eco tableware.
